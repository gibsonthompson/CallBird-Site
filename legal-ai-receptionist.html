The Real Cost of Missed Calls for Law Firms

Lost High-Value Cases

A single personal injury case averages $15,000-50,000 in attorney fees. Miss just 2 PI consultation calls per week because you're in depositions or court, and the math gets ugly fast. Family law cases average $8,000-15,000, estate planning $2,500-5,000. One missed call can equal months of salary.

Reality check: Prospective clients call 3-4 firms before choosing one. If you don't answer, they hire your competitor. It's that simple.

After-Hours Opportunity Loss

42% of legal calls come outside 9-5. People call lawyers when they're stressed — late at night after an arrest, on weekends after receiving divorce papers, early morning before work. If your voicemail says "call back Monday," they're calling the 24/7 firm down the street.

Real impact: Law firms using 24/7 AI receptionists report 3-5 additional case signings monthly from after-hours calls alone.

Time Wasted on Unqualified Leads

60% of law firm calls are unqualified: wrong practice area, jurisdiction conflicts, statute of limitations expired, no damages, or sales calls. If your firm gets 40 calls a day and spends 5 minutes on each unqualified one, that's over 60 hours a month of wasted attorney and staff time.

Solution: AI screens calls with qualifying questions and only forwards cases in your practice areas, within statute of limitations, in proper jurisdiction, and meeting your minimum case value.
