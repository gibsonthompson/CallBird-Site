About

Gibson Thompson is a serial entrepreneur and full-stack developer based in Atlanta, Georgia. He founded CallBird AI to solve a problem he saw across dozens of small businesses: missed phone calls costing thousands in lost revenue every month.

With expertise spanning SaaS architecture, voice AI, and small business operations, Gibson builds the technology behind CallBird's AI receptionist platform — from the natural language processing that handles industry-specific calls to the integrations that connect with existing business tools.

His writing on the CallBird blog draws from hands-on experience building and deploying AI phone systems across industries including dental practices, law firms, home services, restaurants, and veterinary clinics.
