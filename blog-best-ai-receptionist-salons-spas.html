Frequently Asked Questions

Can the AI book appointments with specific stylists?
Yes. You can set up the AI to ask "Do you have a preferred stylist?" and book accordingly. It can also suggest available stylists if the requested one is booked, or add the client to that stylist's waitlist.
Does it work with my salon software?
Most AI receptionists integrate with popular salon software including Square Appointments, Vagaro, Fresha, Boulevard, Mindbody, and Google Calendar. Check specific compatibility before signing up.
Can it handle service questions and pricing?
Yes. You train the AI on your service menu, including descriptions and pricing. It can explain the difference between services, quote prices, and recommend appropriate services based on client needs.
What about spa services like massage and facials?
AI receptionists handle spa bookings the same as salon services. They can capture treatment preferences, note any health considerations (pregnancy, injuries), and book appropriate appointment lengths.
How does it handle existing clients vs. new clients?
The AI can ask if they're a new or returning client. For new clients, it captures full contact information and any relevant history. For existing clients, it can reference their usual services and preferred stylist.
