What an AI Receptionist Handles for Insurance Agencies

An AI receptionist for insurance agencies handles quote requests (capturing coverage type, current carrier, and contact info), initial claims intake (policy number, incident details, date), appointment scheduling with specific agents, general FAQ answering (hours, carriers represented, payment options), and after-hours call capture with instant SMS to the agent.

Quote Request Capture

When a prospect calls asking "How much is homeowners insurance?" the AI captures everything your agent needs for a quote: property address, current carrier and premium, coverage amount, any recent claims, and the best time to call back. Your agent gets an SMS with a warm lead ready to quote instead of a cold voicemail.

Claims Intake

A client calling to report a fender-bender or a tree falling on their roof needs to feel heard immediately. The AI captures their policy number, the date and nature of the incident, whether anyone was injured, and their contact information. It sends an urgent SMS to the agent for follow-up. The client feels taken care of instead of abandoned to voicemail during a stressful moment.

Policy Inquiry Routing

Existing clients call with questions: "When is my policy renewal?" "Can I add my teenage driver?" "What's my deductible?" The AI can answer general questions from your knowledge base and route specific policy inquiries to the right agent, capturing the client's policy number so the agent has context before calling back.

Renewal Season Surge Handling

Renewal seasons create massive call spikes. An agency that normally handles 80 calls per month might get 200 during October/November (auto and home renewal season). CallBird's flat $99/month pricing absorbs this surge without additional cost. Per-call services would charge $159-$299 during your busiest months.

💡 Storm season value: After a major weather event—hailstorm, hurricane, tornado—insurance agency call volume can spike 5-10x overnight. Clients are calling to file claims. Prospects are calling to get coverage they should have had. An AI receptionist ensures every single one of these calls is answered, captured, and routed while your agents focus on the most urgent cases.

After-Hours New Client Capture

Many people shop for insurance in the evening after work. If your office closes at 5pm, you're missing the 6-9pm window when prospects are comparing quotes online and calling agencies. The AI answers these after-hours calls, captures the quote request, and schedules a callback for the next morning. Your agent starts the day with warm leads instead of an empty calendar.
