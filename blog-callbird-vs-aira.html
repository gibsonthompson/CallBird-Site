CallBird vs AIRA: AI Receptionist Comparison [2026]

📅 Mar 14 • ⏱️ 9 min read • By CallBird Team
📌 Quick Answer

CallBird AI ($99-$499/mo) is better for service businesses wanting industry-specific AI templates and emergency detection. AIRA ($24.95-$159.95/mo) is better for budget-conscious businesses wanting the lowest starting price with built-in bilingual support. Both offer strong value under $200/month.

CallBird and AIRA are both newer AI receptionist services competing against established players like Smith.ai and Ruby at significantly lower price points. They take different approaches: CallBird focuses on industry specialization, AIRA focuses on accessibility and bilingual support.
