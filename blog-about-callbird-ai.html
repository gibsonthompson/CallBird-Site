What Does CallBird AI Do?

CallBird AI is an AI receptionist that answers your business phone calls automatically, 24 hours a day. It handles appointment booking, FAQ answering, message taking, emergency detection, and call routing.

CallBird AI replaces voicemail with an intelligent phone agent that actually engages your callers. When someone calls your business and you can't answer—because you're on a job, with a patient, in a meeting, or it's 2am—CallBird picks up instantly, greets them naturally, and handles the conversation.

How CallBird AI Works

CallBird uses speech-to-text, natural language processing, and text-to-speech to have natural phone conversations. Setup takes under 10 minutes—point it at your website and configure your business details.

Step 1: Sign up and enter your business info. Tell CallBird your business name, hours, services, pricing, and any FAQs. The AI can also scrape your website to learn this automatically.

Step 2: Choose your industry template. Select from pre-built AI configurations for home services, medical, legal, salon, real estate, or veterinary. Each template includes industry-specific conversation logic—a plumbing template asks about water damage and urgency; a dental template asks about new vs. existing patient and insurance.

Step 3: Connect your calendar. Link Google Calendar so the AI can book appointments based on your real availability.

Step 4: Forward your calls. Set up call forwarding from your existing business number to your CallBird number. When you can't answer, CallBird picks up.

The entire setup takes under 10 minutes. You can test it by calling your own number before going live.

CallBird AI Key Features

Instant SMS Notifications

Every call generates an instant SMS summary sent to your phone. You see who called, what they needed, and whether it's urgent—within seconds. This is critical for service businesses where calling back a lead within 5 minutes increases conversion rates dramatically.

Emergency Detection & Escalation

CallBird monitors for emergency keywords in real time. When a caller reports a burst pipe, gas leak, electrical fire, or medical emergency, the AI immediately alerts you via SMS and phone call while keeping the caller on the line to capture their address and details.

Website Knowledge Scraping

Point CallBird at your website and it learns your services, pricing, hours, FAQs, and service area automatically. No need to manually enter every detail—though you can override and customize anything.

Calendar Integration

The AI books appointments directly into Google Calendar based on your real-time availability. Callers get confirmed on the spot instead of hearing "someone will call you back."

Call Recording & Transcripts

Every call is recorded and transcribed. Review conversations, train the AI on edge cases, and keep records for compliance or quality purposes.
