What Restaurants Need From AI Phone Answering

Restaurants need AI that handles reservations, answers menu and allergy questions, manages hours and wait times, processes takeout/delivery calls, and handles large party inquiries—all during chaotic peak service hours.

  • Reservation booking: Check availability for the requested date, party size, and time. Confirm or offer alternatives.
  • Menu questions: Answer questions about dishes, ingredients, dietary restrictions, and allergens—especially critical for gluten, nut, and shellfish allergies.
  • Hours and location: These make up 20-30% of all restaurant calls. AI handles them instantly.
  • Large party and event inquiries: These are high-value (private dining can be $5,000-$50,000+). The AI should capture details and flag for management.
  • Takeout and delivery: Process orders or direct callers to your online ordering system.
  • Wait time estimates: During service, provide estimated wait times without distracting your host.
