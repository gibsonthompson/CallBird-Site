Why Electricians Need Specialized AI

Electricians need AI that detects fire and safety hazards (sparking, burning smells, exposed wiring), distinguishes emergency from routine calls, captures panel/breaker details, and dispatches immediately for dangerous situations.

  • Safety triage: "Sparking outlet" requires immediate dispatch. "Want to add a ceiling fan" can wait for a scheduled appointment. The AI needs to know the difference.
  • Job scope capture: Residential vs. commercial, panel age, number of circuits, permit requirements—this info helps you quote accurately before arriving.
  • Code compliance questions: Callers often ask about permits, inspections, and code requirements. The AI should handle these FAQs.
  • Generator and EV charger inquiries: These high-value jobs ($3,000-$10,000+) are increasingly common. The AI should recognize and prioritize these leads.

Electrical Emergency Triage

Immediate dispatch: Sparking outlets/switches, burning electrical smell, exposed/damaged wiring, complete power loss to property, electrical fire signs, downed power line on property.

Same-day response: Partial power loss (some circuits), flickering lights throughout house, breaker that won't reset, outlet not working (only if critical—medical equipment, refrigeration).

Schedule normally: Additional outlet installation, lighting upgrades, ceiling fan installation, panel upgrade quote, EV charger installation, home inspection prep, generator quote.
